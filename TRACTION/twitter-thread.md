## Tweet 3 (Solution)
What CVBER does — the full pipeline:

1️⃣ Upload art → AI scans for theft
2️⃣ C2PA certificate (proves you created it)
3️⃣ Bitcoin blockchain proof (immutable timestamp)
4️⃣ DMCA takedown automation
5️⃣ Court-ready evidence PDF

All free. No credit card. No "trial."
