# Show HN: CVBER – Free, open-source AI art protection (C2PA + DMCA + blockchain)

**Title (93 chars):**
Show HN: CVBER – Free AI-powered art protection (C2PA, DMCA, blockchain proof)

**URL:** https://cvber.vercel.app

**Comment:**

I'm 16 and built this in a weekend because 2.5 billion images are stolen daily by AI training pipelines and artists lose $27B+ a year. Existing tools cost $19-$199/month or take 30-50% commission.

CVBER is free, open-source, and covers the full pipeline:
- **AI Scan** – 12-layer classifier detects if your art has been stolen
- **5-engine reverse image search** – Yandex, Bing, Google Lens, TinEye, SauceNAO
- **Deep search** – NVIDIA NIM describes your image, generates queries, finds matches
- **C2PA certificate** – Cryptographic proof of ownership (recognized by Adobe, Microsoft, Google)
- **Blockchain proof** – Bitcoin OP_RETURN timestamp for immutable evidence
- **Invisible watermarking** – 3 styles, client-side + server-side
- **DMCA takedown** – Automated generator with platform-specific templates
- **Evidence PDF** – Court-ready document combining all proofs

Built with Next.js 15 + Python FastAPI. 54 API endpoints. All on free tiers (Vercel + Render + Supabase).

Before building, I ran 2,400 synthetic interviews across 8 art segments using cognitive behavioral science to find the right beachhead market. Tattoo artists scored 8.5/10 fit.

Would love feedback from the HN community – especially on the C2PA integration and whether the "free forever" model is sustainable.
