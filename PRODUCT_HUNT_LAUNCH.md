# CVBER — Product Hunt Launch Kit

## Tagline
**Protect your art from AI theft — free C2PA certificates, DMCA automation, and 24/7 monitoring.**

## Description (for Product Hunt)

### TL;DR
CVBER is a free AI-powered art protection platform that gives digital artists C2PA provenance certificates, automated DMCA takedown generation, and real-time theft monitoring. It's the Glaze/Nightshade alternative that handles the legal side.

### For whom?
Digital artists, photographers, illustrators, NFT creators, and anyone whose art is being scraped by AI companies without permission.

### What does it do?
1. **C2PA Certificates** — Cryptographic proof of ownership embedded in your files. Same standard as Adobe, Microsoft, Google.
2. **DMCA Automation** — Auto-generates legally formatted takedown notices when your art is stolen.
3. **24/7 Monitoring** — Scans Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, DeviantArt, Reddit for unauthorized copies.
4. **Blockchain Attestation** — Bitcoin-anchored proof of creation via OpenTimestamps.

### How is it different?
Glaze/Nightshade protect your art technically. CVBER protects it legally. Many artists use both together.

### How do I get started?
1. Go to cvber.vercel.app
2. Sign up (free, no credit card)
3. Upload your art
4. Get your C2PA certificate
5. Turn on monitoring
