> "Have you ever found your art being used without your permission? What happened?"

## Phase 4: Interview Script

### Pre-Interview Setup
- Record the call (with permission)
- Take notes on emotional responses
- Note body language and tone

### Opening (2 minutes)

> "Thanks for doing this. I'm building a free tool to protect artists from art theft. Before I build more features, I want to understand how artists actually deal with this. There are no right or wrong answers — I just want to hear your honest experience. Can I record this for my notes?"

### Part 1: Their Story (10 minutes)

**Q1: Walk me through what happened.**
> "Can you tell me about a time your art was stolen or used without permission? What happened?"

*Listen for: Emotional response, specific details, how they found out*

**Q2: How did it make you feel?**
> "How did that make you feel? Walk me through the emotions."

*Listen for: Anger, helplessness, grief, resignation — compare to our emotional arc model*

**Q3: What did you do about it?**
> "What did you do next? Did you try to get it taken down?"

*Listen for: Specific actions taken, tools used, outcomes*

### Part 2: What They've Tried (10 minutes)

**Q4: What tools have you used?**
> "Have you used any tools or services to protect your art? What were they? What worked? What didn't?"

*Listen for: Pixsy, DMCA.com, TinEye, manual DMCA, nothing — compare to our competitor analysis*

**Q5: What was frustrating?**
> "What was frustrating about those tools? What was missing?"

*Listen for: Pain points that match our feature gap analysis*

**Q6: What would you pay for?**
> "If there was a tool that did everything you wanted, what would you be willing to pay for it?"

*Listen for: WTP range — compare to our $14.20/mo average*

### Part 3: Testing Our Findings (10 minutes)

**Q7: "Antivirus for art" concept**
> "If there was a tool that monitored and protected your art automatically — like antivirus for your art — what would that look like? What would it do?"

*Validate Finding 1: 68% independently described this concept*

**Q8: Proof before trust**
> "What would you need to see before signing up for a new protection tool? What would convince you it's real?"

*Validate Finding 2: 91% demanded proof before trust*

**Q9: Free suspicion**
> "How do you feel about tools that are 'free'? What concerns come up when you hear 'free'?"

*Validate Finding 3: 78% flagged free = suspicious*

**Q10: Emotional cost**
> "When your art was stolen, was it more about the money or something else? What was the real cost?"

*Validate Finding 4: 62% said emotional cost > financial*

**Q11: Global enforcement**
> "If someone in another country steals your art, what do you do? What would you want to happen?"

*Validate Finding 5: 45% cited global enforcement gap*

### Part 4: Product Feedback (5 minutes)

**Q12: Show them CVBER**
> "I'm building a tool called CVBER. It does [brief description]. What's your first reaction?"

*Listen for: Immediate concerns, excitement, skepticism*

**Q13: What's missing?**
> "What's missing? What would make you actually use this?"

*Listen for: Feature requests that match or contradict our data*

**Q14: What would hold you back?**
> "What would hold you back from using this? What's the biggest barrier?"

*Listen for: Trust barriers that match our findings*

### Closing (3 minutes)

**Q15: Anything else?**
> "Is there anything else I should know? Anything I didn't ask about?"

**Q16: Can I follow up?**
> "Can I follow up with you if I have more questions? And can I use your story (anonymized) in my research?"

**Q17: Thank you**
> "Thank you so much. I really appreciate your time. I'll send you that gift card now."

## Phase 5: What to Validate

### The 5 Findings We're Testing

| # | Finding | Synthetic Result | Real Validation Criteria |
|---|---------|-----------------|------------------------|
| 1 | "Antivirus for art" | 68% (816/1,200) | If 4/5+ artists independently describe set-and-forget protection → **Validated** |
| 2 | Proof before trust | 91% (1,092/1,200) | If 4/5+ artists say they need case studies before signing up → **Validated** |
| 3 | Free = suspicious | 78% (936/1,200) | If 4/5+ artists express suspicion about "free" → **Validated** |
| 4 | Emotional > financial | 62% (744/1,200) | If 3/5+ artists say emotional cost matters more than money → **Validated** |
| 5 | Global enforcement gap | 45% (540/1,200) | If 2/5+ artists cite international enforcement as a problem → **Validated** |

### Success Criteria

| Result | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 80%+ confirm (4/5+ findings) | **Fully validated** — use in Techstars pitch |
| 60-80% confirm (3/5 findings) | **Partially validated** — refine messaging |
| <60% confirm (<3 findings) | **Invalidated** — rethink approach |
