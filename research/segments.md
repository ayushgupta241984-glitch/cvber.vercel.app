# CVBER Research — Art Segments & Personas

## 8 Target Segments

Each segment represents a different type of creator CVBER serves.

---

### Segment 1: Freelance Illustrator
**Who:** Independent artists selling commissions, merch, prints
**Income:** $20K-$60K/yr
**Platforms:** Instagram, Twitter, DeviantArt, personal site
**Pain level:** HIGH — had art stolen, knows it, angry about it
**Budget for protection:** $0-$20/month
**Tech comfort:** Medium — uses social media, basic tools

**Persona variations:**
- Freelancer who had art stolen on Pinterest/WeHeartIt
- Freelancer worried about AI training but never had theft
- Freelancer who tried DMCA takedowns and failed
- Freelancer who uses AI tools themselves (conflicted)

---

### Segment 2: Digital Painter / Concept Artist
**Who:** Professional-quality digital paintings, fan art, original work
**Income:** $40K-$100K/yr (mix of commissions + employment)
**Platforms:** ArtStation, Instagram, Twitter, personal portfolio
**Pain level:** MEDIUM-HIGH — aware of AI, concerned about style theft
**Budget for protection:** $0-$50/month
**Tech comfort:** High — uses Photoshop, Procreate, knows file formats

**Persona variations:**
- Concept artist at a studio worried about style being cloned
- Independent painter selling prints on Etsy
- Fan artist whose work gets reposted without credit
- Artist who got viral and now fears mass scraping

---

### Segment 3: Photographer
**Who:** Professional and semi-professional photographers
**Income:** $30K-$80K/yr
**Platforms:** Instagram, Flickr, 500px, personal site
**Pain level:** HIGH — photo theft is rampant, stock photo abuse
**Budget for protection:** $10-$50/month
**Tech comfort:** Medium-High — knows metadata, EXIF, watermarks

**Persona variations:**
- Wedding photographer whose images appear on stock sites
- Landscape photographer finding work on print-on-demand
- Street photographer concerned about facial recognition
- Commercial photographer protecting client work

---

### Segment 4: Graphic Designer
**Who:** Logo, brand, UI/UX designers
**Income:** $35K-$90K/yr
**Platforms:** Dribbble, Behance, Instagram, LinkedIn
**Pain level:** MEDIUM — designs get copied, but more concerned about client work
**Budget for protection:** $10-$100/month (client budgets)
**Tech comfort:** High — Adobe suite, Figma, knows file structures

**Persona variations:**
- Freelance logo designer whose work gets traced
- UI designer worried about design system theft
- Brand designer protecting client identities
- Motion graphics artist protecting animations

---

### Segment 5: Comic / Manga Artist
**Who:** Sequential artists, webtoon creators
**Income:** $15K-$70K/yr (Patreon, commissions, publications)
**Platforms:** Webtoon, Tapas, Twitter, Instagram, Pixiv
**Pain level:** VERY HIGH — piracy, panel theft, AI style cloning
**Budget for protection:** $0-$30/month
**Tech comfort:** Medium — specialized tools (Clip Studio, Medibang)

**Persona variations:**
- Webtoon creator whose panels are reposted on piracy sites
- Manga artist worried about AI generating in their style
- Comic artist who lost a Patreon due to stolen content
- Fan comic artist in legal gray area

---

### Segment 6: 3D Artist / Game Dev
**Who:** 3D modelers, environment artists, character artists
**Income:** $30K-$120K/yr
**Platforms:** ArtStation, Sketchfab, TurboSquid, personal site
**Pain level:** HIGH — 3D models get ripped and redistributed
**Budget for protection:** $20-$100/month
**Tech comfort:** Very High — Blender, Maya, knows file encryption

**Persona variations:**
- Game dev whose assets get ripped from games
- 3D modeler selling on TurboSquid, finds stolen copies
- Environment artist for studios worried about leaks
- NFT artist (post-crash, still creating)

---

### Segment 7: Tattoo Artist
**Who:** Custom tattoo designers, flash artists
**Income:** $25K-$80K/yr
**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, personal portfolios
**Pain level:** HIGH — designs get stolen and used by other tattoo shops
**Budget for protection:** $0-$20/month
**Tech comfort:** Low-Medium — uses Instagram mainly

**Persona variations:**
- Custom tattoo artist whose designs appear at other shops
- Flash artist finding work on knockoff print sites
- Tattoo artist worried about AI generating their flash
- Shop owner protecting multiple artists' portfolios

---

### Segment 8: AI-Assisted Creator
**Who:** Artists who use AI tools in their workflow
**Income:** $10K-$60K/yr
**Platforms:** Midjourney Discord, Twitter, Instagram
**Pain level:** COMPLICATED — uses AI but also worried about AI
**Budget for protection:** $0-$30/month
**Tech comfort:** Very High — early adopter, understands AI models

**Persona variations:**
- Artist who uses AI for reference but creates final work manually
- Prompt engineer who generates art commercially
- Artist conflicted about AI ethics but needs to compete
- Artist who trained a model on their own style
