## VERDICT: CVBER Works — But Not For Everyone Yet

**Overall Product-Market Fit: 7.6/10**

The product has strong fit with Tattoo Artists (8.5), Comic/Manga Artists (8.5), and Digital Painters (7.7). Weak fit with AI-Assisted Creators (6.7) and Photographers (6.9). The core problem is real. The solution is desired. But adoption barriers are significant.

## 3. TRUST — The #1 Adoption Barrier

### Top Trust Barriers (Cross-Segment)

| Barrier | Segments Citing | Impact |
|---------|----------------|--------|
| **AI training on uploaded art** | 7/8 | CRITICAL — "Will you train AI on my art?" |
| **Data privacy / breach risk** | 6/8 | HIGH — "What if you get hacked?" |
| **Platform longevity** | 5/8 | HIGH — "What if you shut down?" |
| **Upload speed / file size** | 4/8 | MEDIUM — Large files take time |
| **Copyright standing (AI art)** | 3/8 | MEDIUM — AI creators uncertain about own rights |

### Trust Solutions (What Would Help)

1. **Open source the code** — 68% of interviews mentioned this as trust builder
2. **Zero-knowledge encryption** — "Only I can see my art"
3. **No AI training promise** — Explicit, enforceable, public commitment
4. **Data export / delete** — "I can leave anytime"
5. **Case studies** — Real artists who recovered stolen work
